About Gatiman Castings

Gatiman Castings LLP is a premier manufacturer specializing in high-quality iron and aluminum castings. With decades of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses requiring precision casting components.

Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped with the latest technology and operated by skilled craftsmen who combine traditional expertise with modern manufacturing techniques.

  • Advanced casting facilities with cutting-edge technology
  • Comprehensive quality control at every production stage
  • Skilled team with decades of combined industry experience
  • On-time delivery and exceptional customer service

Our Services

We offer comprehensive casting solutions tailored to meet the specific requirements of various industries. Our services include:

Iron Casting

Aluminum Casting
